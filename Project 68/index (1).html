Campus Life

School Grass Fields

Was created in 1968 along with the original design of the school

The grass fields of Bonita Vista Middle holds many different events, such as soccer tournaments, track and field competitions, football tournaments, softball tournaments, and more. Its gigantic area helps many students in P.E. and staying fit.



School Library

Was created in 1968 along with the original design of the school

The Bonita Vista Middle library has seen its fair share of use, hosting thousands of books, the library club, and all of its students. Books held inside of the library range from every genre and every subject. Fiction, Educational, Narrative, whatever you name it, we have.
